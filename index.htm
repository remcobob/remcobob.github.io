ROBERT MESICK'S HOME PAGE

 

Here, I cover the things I am interested in, the places I've been, and the work I've done. It has an extensive health and wellness section. People have asked me what I do (did) for a living but they never understand because you have to see it to relate.

In the "Health and Wellness" section I cover spices and anti-inflammatory plants and where to get quality information regarding quality health care and medicines.

Under "How NOT to Die" is diet research and data. There are links to various sources. It will be big.

Under "Remco Engineering" there is the old Rremco Engineering web site and a section with pictures of some the systems I designed and manufactured with their locations. I had a small business (very small) and built some really neat (and big) systems. The story is about timing, luck, liking to do research and figuring out how to live in the cracks.  This is really for my grandkids someday.

GitHub is a free website with no renewal issues so this should be around for a long time.
